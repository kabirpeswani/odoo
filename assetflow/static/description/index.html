AssetFlow - Enterprise Asset & Resource Management System Track, allocate, and maintain physical assets and shared resources through a centralized ERP platform. Key Features: - Department, Asset Category, and Employee Directory management - Full asset lifecycle tracking (Available, Allocated, Reserved, Under Maintenance, Lost, Retired, Disposed) - Asset allocation with double-allocation prevention and transfer workflows - Shared resource booking with overlap validation - Maintenance request approval workflow - Scheduled audit cycles with discrepancy reporting - Dashboard KPIs, notifications, and activity logs - Role-based access control
